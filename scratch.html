About the Seattle Mariners

The Seattle Mariners are a professional baseball team based in Seattle, Washington. They are a member of the American League (AL) West division in Major League Baseball (MLB).

Team Achievements:

  1. Division Championships: 3
  2. American League Pennants: 0
  3. World Series Championships: 0

Notable Players:

  • Ken Griffey Jr.
  • Ichiro Suzuki
  • Felix Hernandez
